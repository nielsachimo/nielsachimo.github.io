Rock Paper Scissors

Instructions

Hey guys, and welcome to Rock Paper Scissors or simply RPS. If this is your first time playing don't worry, the game is very simple and I'll guide you through the process.

RPS is like a war game, and to fight it you have to choose 1 of 3 weapons (this is kind of a silly analogy, but most people understand it better this way). Each weapon has it's own strengths and weaknesses.


Rock

It's a great weapon to use against scissor because you can usually smash and literally destroy the scissor, but when it finds paper it tends to be very weak because the paper is used to wrap the rock and eliminate it's destructive powers.

Paper

It's great against rock because it wraps it makes the rock useless, but a bad weapon to use against scissor. Why? Because it can literally cut the paper into pieces.

Scissor

Great weapon against paper, it really makes paper look like garbash, but scissor fails miserably against rock as it gets completely destroyed.

When you start the game, you'll have to choose how many games you want to play. This is mainly all there is to it, as I told you at first, it's a very simple game!

If this wasn't enough to make you understand try playing a few games, I'm sure you'll get the grasp of it! To return HOME (homepage) click on the header (that place on the top with a big Rock Paper Scissors... did you see the color changing? cool right =D) or just click here.
